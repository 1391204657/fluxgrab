FAQ

Good to know

What can I download with FluxGrab?

FluxGrab is a general-purpose tool for saving media you own, created, or have explicit permission or a license to download — for example your own uploads, content released under permissive licenses, or files you're authorized to keep for offline use.

Which sites can I download videos from?

FluxGrab works with 1,000+ sites. The free online downloader handles TikTok, X (Twitter), Instagram and other social platforms right in your browser, while the desktop app adds YouTube, high-quality 1080p/4K and many more sources — for media you own or are licensed to download.

Is this legal?

The tool itself is legal. You are responsible for how you use it. Please only download content you have the right to, and always respect the terms of service of the sites you use and applicable copyright law. See our Terms.

Do I need an account?

No. The free plan works without an account. A Pro license simply unlocks higher quality and batch features on your device.

Is Pro really one-time?

Yes. $19.9 once, lifetime license with free updates. No recurring charges.

What's the difference between Free and Pro?

Free lets you save up to 720p, a couple of downloads a day, no account needed. Pro unlocks 1080p/4K, unlimited downloads, batch/playlist downloading and MP3 extraction — all running entirely on your own machine.

Yes — see our Refund Policy. If FluxGrab doesn't work for you, contact us within the stated window for a refund.
