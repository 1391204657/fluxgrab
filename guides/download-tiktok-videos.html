How to download TikTok videos

Updated July 2026

FluxGrab lets you save TikTok videos as MP4 files from your browser — no app install required for basic downloads. Only download content you have the right to keep.

Steps (online)

  1. Open the TikTok video and tap Share → Copy link.
  2. Visit the TikTok downloader page.
  3. Paste the URL and click Download.
  4. Save the MP4 when your browser prompts you.

Tips

  • Public videos work best. Private or region-locked content may fail.
  • For batch downloads or higher limits, use the Windows desktop app.
  • Video quality depends on what TikTok exposes for that link.
