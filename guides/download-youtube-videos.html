How to download YouTube videos (up to 4K)

Updated July 2026

YouTube HD uses DASH — separate audio and video streams. FluxGrab merges them locally on your PC for clean MP4 files up to 4K. This requires the desktop app, not the online-only tool.

Why the desktop app?

Browser-based downloaders cannot reliably merge YouTube 1080p/4K streams. FluxGrab runs yt-dlp locally and merges with FFmpeg for the best quality without re-encoding when possible.

Steps

  1. Download FluxGrab for Windows and unzip.
  2. Copy the YouTube video URL.
  3. Paste into FluxGrab or use the YouTube preview page first.
  4. Pick quality — free plan: 2 downloads/day up to 720p; Pro: unlimited 4K.

Pricing

  • Free: 2 downloads per day, up to 720p.
  • Pro ($19.9 lifetime): unlimited downloads, 4K, MP3 export.
