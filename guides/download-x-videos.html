How to download X (Twitter) videos

Updated July 2026

FluxGrab lets you save videos from public X (Twitter) posts as MP4. Works with x.com, twitter.com, and many t.co links. Only download content you have the right to keep.

Steps (online)

  1. Open the post that contains the video on X.
  2. Tap Share → Copy link, or copy the URL from the browser address bar.
  3. Visit the X video downloader page.
  4. Paste the URL and click Download.
  5. Save the MP4 when your browser prompts you.

Tips

  • Public posts work best. Protected accounts and some age-restricted posts may fail.
  • If the link ends with /video/1, FluxGrab still accepts it — you can also use the main post URL.
  • For batch downloads or higher limits, use the Windows desktop app.
