How to download Instagram Reels and videos

Updated July 2026

FluxGrab can save public Instagram Reels, feed videos, and many post videos as MP4 from your browser. Only download content you own or are licensed to keep.

Steps (online)

  1. Open the Reel or video post in Instagram (app or web).
  2. Tap ⋯ or Share → Copy link.
  3. Visit the Instagram downloader page.
  4. Paste the URL and click Download.
  5. Save the MP4 when your browser prompts you.

What works / what may fail

  • Works best: public Reels and public video posts.
  • May fail: private accounts, Stories, login-walled content, or posts Instagram temporarily blocks from third-party fetchers.
  • If parsing fails, try again later or use the Windows desktop app.

Tips

  • Prefer the full instagram.com/reel/… or /p/… link (not only a share sheet preview).
  • Quality depends on what Instagram exposes for that link (often up to 720p online).
  • For batch downloads or higher limits, use the desktop app.
